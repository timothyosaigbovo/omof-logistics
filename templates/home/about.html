About Omof Logistics

Delivering excellence in logistics since inception

Our Story

Omof Logistics is a Lagos-based logistics company providing end-to-end freight forwarding, shipping, customs clearance, and supply chain management services. We serve businesses across Africa and beyond, delivering reliable and efficient logistics solutions.

Our extensive logistics solutions are crafted to adapt to the ever-changing needs of businesses across different sectors. We focus on combining reliability, innovation, and efficiency throughout the entire supply chain.

Moving Your Business Forward

Our Mission

To provide world-class logistics services that empower businesses to reach global markets efficiently, safely, and affordably. We are committed to being the bridge that connects African businesses to the world.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and innovative logistics partner, setting new standards for reliability, technology-driven solutions, and customer satisfaction in the freight and shipping industry.
